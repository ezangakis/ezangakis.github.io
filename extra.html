Let's Play Simon!


The rules are simple. Simon will tell you a series of buttons to press. When he's done, repeat them backt to him by clicking them in the same order they were flashed to you! Simon starts off by flashing one button, and for every successful repetition, he'll repeat his previous button flashes and add one more flash at the end. Get started and test your memory!
